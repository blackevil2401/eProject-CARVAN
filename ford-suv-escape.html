EXTERIOR

Capability to Explore



It’s a big world out there. Escape lets you hit the road and explore it with the choice of three different engines. For helping keeping you in control, there’s also the available All-Wheel Drive Disconnect and standard Selectable Drive Modes.





INTERIOR

Comfort and Convenience.



Its interior has also been redesigned with all the features and conveniences to help make traveling, and exploring, more comfortable. A new rotary gear shift dial opens space on the center console and helps improve overall front-row comfort.





FEATURE

TECHNOLOGY
Innovative


Operate


Connect & Control

Access complimentary remote features, like start/stop, lock/unlock, schedule a start, locate vehicle and vehicle status check on the FordPass App when your vehicle us equipped with FordPass Connect.


Voice-Activated Connectivity

2020 Escape features an available eight-inch voice-activated capacitative touchscreen with pinch-to-zoom capability.


Wireless Charging Pad

Stay powered on the go with the available Wireless Charging Pad located in the center console. Just set your smartphone, charging side down, on top of the pad and it automatically senses and begins charging the device.


FordPass Connect Wifi

With FordPass Connect, Ford owners and passengers can enjoy an available 4G LTE in vehicle Wi-Fi hotspot, powered by AT&T.


Safe


BLIS® with Cross-Traffic Alert

BLIS® with Cross-Traffic Alert uses radar to identify if a vehicle is detected in your blind spot and alerts you with an indicator light in the appropriate exterior mirror


Lane-Keeping System

The Lane-Keeping System can help you stay in your lane, even during a moment of distraction. This smart feature scans your vehicle's position between the lines in the road ahead and can alert you if you're starting to edge out of your lane.


Ford Co-Pilot360™

Ford Co-Pilot360™ is an advanced suite of standard driver-assist technologies that includes BLIS® with Cross-Traffic Alert, Pre-Collision Assist with Automatic Emergency Braking, Auto High-Beam Headlamps, Lane-Keeping System and Rear View Camera.


Rear View Camera

The Rear View Camera provides a great view of what's behind your vehicle. Just make sure to keep the camera lends free of debris and you can navigate your vehicle in Reverse with confidence.
