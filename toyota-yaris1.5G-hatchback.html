Salient features


Operate


Gear

Infinitely automatic transmission with 7 virtual gears helps the car operate smoothly, smooth acceleration, without feeling jerky when gearing up.


Engine

The new Yaris is powered by a 2NR-FE engine with electronic fuel injection technology and Dual VVTi intelligent variable valve timing.


Safe


Electronic brake force distribution system

The EBD distributes the braking force properly to the wheels, helping to improve
braking efficiency, especially when the
vehicle is loaded and cornering.


Anti-lock braking system

Anti-lock braking system (ABS) controls the brake by computer to prevent the wheel from slipping when braking. If a stiffening occurs at a wheel, the system will release the brake to prevent the wheel from locking.


Electronic balance

The VCS controls the engine power and properly distributes the braking force to each wheel, thereby minimizing the risk of losing control and slipping, especially on slippery roads or when cornering.


Structured seats reduce neck injuries

The headrests are equipped with thicker foam cushions, which help to press the passenger's head and neck to minimize cervical vertebrae injury when a collision occurs.
