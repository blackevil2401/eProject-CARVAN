Safe



Blind Spot Collision Warning
(BCW)

Blind Spot Collision Warning (BCW) is advanced technology that helps reduce the blind spots on the side and rear areas of your Kia. The radar-based detection system sends out visual or auditory signals to help warn you of objects not in your field of view.



Parking Distance Warning-Reverse
(PDW-R)

The Parking Distance Warning-Reverse (PDW-R) uses a sonar sensor system to detect obstacles behind your Optima. If a hazard is detected, the system provides an audible warning.



Rear Cross Traffic Collision Warning
(RCCW)

An added feature of Blind Spot Collision Warning (BCW), radar sensors hidden in the corners of the rear bumper scan for passing cars, motorcycles, and/or other vehicles as you back up. If the system detects a vehicle crossing into your intended path, a warning sounds and a light appears on the left or right outside rear view mirror, depending on the location of the other vehicle.



Forward Collision Warning
(FCW)

Forward Collision Warning (FCW) employs front-mounted radar to detect a potential collision with the car in front, and provides the driver with audible warnings and visual signals on the meter display.
