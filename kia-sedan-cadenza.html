Safe



Forward Collision Avoidance Assistance – Junction Turning (FCA-JT)

The standard FCA-JT system can assist the driver in braking to help prevent or
lessen the effects of a collision in certain conditions when turning left while an oncoming vehicle is approaching.



Blind Spot Collision Avoidance – Rear (BCA-R)

If you try to change lanes while an object is detected in the blind spot,
BCA-R can apply brake pressure to the front wheels to help maintain your previous course.



Safe Exit Assist (SEA)

If necessary, standard Safe Exit Assist (SEA) will override attempts to deactivate the Electronic Child Safety Lock
until it’s safe for your rear passengers to open and exit the vehicle.



Dual Front and Rear Seat-Mounted Side Airbags

When used with seat belts, the dual front seat-mounted side airbags offer an additional level of enhanced safety
and can be effective at reducing injuries from certain collisions.
