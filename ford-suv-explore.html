TECHNOLOGY
Enjoy the journey.


Operate


SYNC® 3

SYNC 3 is our easy-to-use, responsive voice-activated technology. Experience the available 10.1-inch touchscreen with impressive responsiveness.


Wireless Charging

The days of taking turns to charge devices are over. Explorer offers up to nine different powerpoints, including USBs, USCs, 12V, 110V and regular plugs - which means plenty of power for every passenger.


FordPass Connect with 4G LTE wifi hotspot

With FordPass Connect, drivers and passengers can enjoy an in-vehicle Wifi hotspot. It features a 4G LTE wifi hotspot, powered by AT&T.


B&O Sound System with 14 Speakers

Engjoy the ride with this available B&O Sound System by Bang and Olufsen. This 14-speaker, 980-watt system is available with the Premium Technology Pakage.


Safe


Blind Spot Information System with Cross-Traffic Alert
(BLIS)

This feature can help detect and alert you to vehicles in your blind spot, while Cross-Traffic Alert can detect traffic behind you when you're slowly backing out of a parking spot or driveway.


Lane-Keeping System

The Lane-Keeping System can help you stay in your lane, even during a moment of distraction. This smart feature scans your vehicle's position between the lines in the road ahead and can alert you if you're starting to edge out of your lane.


Intelligent Adaptive Cruise Control

Not only does Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) with Stop-and-Go help you keep pace on your journey, it can also slow down your vehicle if the traffic ahead has stopped or slowed.


Rear View Camera

Backing up made easy, the Rear View Camera provides a great view of what's behind your vehicle. It also comes with a built-in washer so you can keep the camera lens free of debris and navigare in Reverse with confidence.
