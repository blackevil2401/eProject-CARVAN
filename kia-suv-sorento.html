Safe


Blind-Spot Collision Avoidance
(BCA)

Available BCA is designed to help detect vehicles in your blind spot in certain situations, including when you change lanes, and may apply the brakes to help mitigate a collision.


Forward Collision-Avoidance Assist with Pedestrian Detection
(FCA)

Available FCA is designed to apply the brakes if the system senses a possible impact, potentially helping to reduce the effects of an impact.


Rear Cross-Traffic Collision Avoidance
(RCCA)

When in reverse, available RCCA can warn you if it detects oncoming obstacles, and may apply the brakes to help mitigate a collision.


Surround View Monitor
(SVM)

Standard on Sorento SX, SVM gives you a virtual bird’s eye view of your relation to surroundings.
