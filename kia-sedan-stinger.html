FEATURE

TECHNOLOGY
All systems go.


Operate



Supervision with 7-inch TFT Display

The available 7-inch Thin Film Transistor (TFT) digital gauges show your vehicle and driving information with outstanding clarity-allowing you to monitor everything from cornering g-force to turbocharger boost level.



Head Up Display (HUD)

The available HUD projects important driving information directly onto the windshield in your line of sight,
to help you stay informed and focused while enjoying the drive.



Shift-By-Wire

The Stinger’s available shift-by-wire gear selector lets you change transmission modes through electronic controls rather than a mechanical linkage,
for modern gear selection and a more refined drive.



Harman Kardon® Speakers

The available 720-watt Harman Kardon® Premium Audio system with Clari-Fi™ provides advanced sound technology that fills the cabin with rich,
balanced audio through 15 speakers.


Safe



Forward Collision Avoidance Assist with Pedestrian Detection
(FCA-Ped)

Available FCA-Ped is designed to help detect a potential collision and help automatically apply the brakes in a variety of circumstances.



Surround View Monitor
(SVM)

Available SVM uses four cameras to help provide a view around the vehicle—especially helpful when parking in tight spots.



Blind-Spot Collision Warning
(BCW)

Available BCW is designed to help detect vehicles in your blind spot,
and provide visible and audible alerts to help you avoid some hazardous lane-change decisions.



Lane Keeping Assist
(LKA)

Available LKA can apply steering input to help maintain course if it detects your Stinger getting too close to lane markers.
